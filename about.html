Introduction

Jason has been studying and working in photography for over 20 years. He has a sports photography background and is very quick to catch action and special moments. He spent many years learning the art of portrait photography and knows how to use studio and natural lighting to enhance the drama and emotion of the subject at hand. He has many years experience in a variety of photography situations including, but not limited to, sporting events, family portraits, high school senior portraits, infant and babies, parties and events, weddings, pet portraits, product photography, and much more.
